My story

I started out as a backend/full-stack developer and worked my way through tech lead, and architect roles before becoming a multi-team engineering leader. Today I guide five teams and more than twenty engineers building the flight and crew systems of a major European airline — software that never sleeps. Together we’ve shipped 15+ products to the Azure cloud on a modern, event-driven architecture.

I was building production AI long before it was cool: I architected conversational AI platform at airline-group scale — multiple chatbots and voice experiences serving travellers on social platforms. Over time, I noticed a pattern: the teams that thrive are the ones where people feel trusted to own their work. That belief shapes how I lead, and how I think about generative AI today — it should be led by people who have actually shipped it.
